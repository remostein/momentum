ABOUT

Looking back on the day/month or year you had in a way that dynamically demonstrates what you experienced through a certain period. From various moments put together you understand your momentum better. Momentum lets you write down all the important things that are happening to you, all the life changing moments, all the moments you want to track, all the moments that made you feel good, and all the moment you regret. Later, Momentum will show you statistical analysis of all your moments, analyzed by word / moments / dates / good-vibes levels and more.

More features will come soon.
